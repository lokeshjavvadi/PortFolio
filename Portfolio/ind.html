2048 GAME

2048 is a simple yet addictive single-player sliding puzzle game. The objective is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to combine them into tiles with higher values, ultimately reaching the coveted '2048' tile. Players strategically merge tiles by matching numbers, doubling their value with each successful combination. The game challenges logical thinking, planning, and problem-solving skills, making it both engaging and mentally stimulating
